2.  **Reduction in Property, Plant, and Equipment (PPE) (Secondary Driver):**
    *   Property, Plant, and Equipment (PPE) net decreased from SAR 115.12 billion in 2023 to SAR 112.58 billion in 2024. This constitutes a reduction of approximately SAR 2.54 billion, which is about 2.21% of the 2023 PPE balance.

*   **Current Assets:** There was a minor decrease in current assets from SAR 100.6 billion in 2023 to SAR 91.4 billion in 2024, a reduction of SAR 9.2 billion.
*   **Investments:** This was partially offset by an increase in investments in associates and joint ventures, which rose from SAR 85.2 billion to SAR 87.8 billion (SAR 2.6 billion increase).

**Conclusion on Inaccuracies:**
No material inaccuracies were identified. The internal data accurately reflects the primary drivers and general magnitudes.
